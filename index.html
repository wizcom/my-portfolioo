About Me

Hey there! I'm Chimango Mughogho 👋 — a curious and motivated Computer Science student at Chancellor College, University of Malawi. I scored 14 points in my 2024 MSCE exams, and though I once planned to study Medical Laboratory Science, life led me into tech — and I haven’t looked back since.

Studying Computer Science has opened my eyes to a world of possibilities — from building systems and solving real-world problems to exploring innovation through code. I'm passionate about technology and eager to keep learning, growing, and one day making a meaningful impact through my work.

Beyond academics, I’m on a mission to inspire others, especially young Africans, to embrace tech and see it as a tool for change.
